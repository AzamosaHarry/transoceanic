Our Projects

3 Metric Tonnes Per Annum FLNG

Transoceanic Gas & Power is thrilled to introduce the Transoceanic FLNG Project, aimed at the development of over 3 million metric tons per annum (MTPA) of LNG. The project will secure its feedstock from various fields located in the swamp and shallow water regions near OPL 289. This model serves to mitigate upstream gas resourcing risks, and we provide a firm commitment of no less than 7 trillion cubic feet (TCF) of gas supply.

Learn more

1,000 Gigawatts Power Barge

Transoceanic Gas & Power Limited introduces a distinctive and risk-mitigated investment opportunity tailored for astute investors, with a strategic focus on capitalizing on the ongoing enhancements in transmission and distribution infrastructure within the Power sector. The project is set to unfold in two distinct phases: Phase 1 will contribute 500MW of power to the grid, and Phase 2 will see an expansion of capacity to 1000MW.

Sustainability

Transoceanic Gas & Power Limited is at the helm, taking the lead with a resolute dedication to sustainability.

Our initial and foremost action in this endeavor is to establish our emission baseline. This will be achieved through the creation of our Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) framework and the comprehensive assessment of our company-wide Greenhouse Gas inventory.

The integration of our ESG framework will lead to the accurate reporting of these efforts in our annual reports and financial statements.
